“Honimal - Rise of the Fusian War” is an independent 3D-action-game, developed by an international team of students.This game is the first introduction into the vast world of “Honimal”, imagined by Nguyen Duc Tri.

This game is currrently in pre-alpha phase.

Honimal is a world in which exist Fusians, creatures with both human and animal traits. The player embodies Chien Ho, a tiger-warrior who must fight to free the other Fusians from slavery instaured by the tyrant Dai Go.
